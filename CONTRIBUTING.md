# Contributing to Lattik Studio

Thanks for your interest in contributing!

## Contributor License Agreement

Before we can accept your contribution, you need to sign our [Contributor License Agreement](CLA.md). This grants Datability LLC the rights needed to distribute your contribution as part of the project, while you retain ownership of your work.

You only need to sign once — your signature applies to all future contributions to this repository.

When you open a pull request, our CLA bot will automatically check your status and post a comment with a link to sign if needed. Pull requests cannot be merged until the CLA is signed.

## Submitting changes

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch
3. Make your changes
4. Open a pull request against `main`
5. Sign the CLA when prompted (first-time contributors)

See [CLAUDE.md](CLAUDE.md) (the project's developer guide) for setup, architecture, and development workflow.
